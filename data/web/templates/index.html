Remax Realestate CALGARY

You want to buy a new home or apartment. You have contacted a real estate company to provide you with a detailed copy of the current listings in that city. Unfortunately, the copies provided only contain the current prices of the house and apartments but gives no indication of the walk, transit, and bike scores to nearby amenities. You would like the information provided to include ratings based on the distances to amenities in each category. This might pose a slight problem because you want to have all route options available to you and at anytime you need. You decide to get down to some digging by performing an ETL and Javascript challenge on two websites; ReMax and Walk Score.
